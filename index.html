Our Services

What we are best at - expert residential & commercial cleaning

Standard Clean

Vacuuming and mopping the floors Cleaning bathrooms: sink, mirror, bath and toilet Tidying up Dusting Cleaning the kitchen: wiping down work surfaces, cabinets, cupboard doors, etc.

Deep Clean

"The Deep Clean" includes "The Standard Clean" with emphasis and focus on build up, plus the following: Carpet: Spot clean stains on carpet and upholstery. Windows: Vacuum windowsills and window tracks Wipe down doors and door frames Garbage Cans (Wipe out and sanitize garbage cans) Blinds Couch & Chairs Microwave and other appliances. We do NOT offer: Carpet cleaning/stain removal beyond vacuuming and minor stains Fire and water damage restoration Defrosting and cleaning freezers Dishwasher cleaning Shower curtain cleaning Small appliance repair Unclogging drains

Office Cleaning

This package involves cleaning and sanitizing commercial buildings. sweeping, mopping, vacuuming carpeted areas, dusting surfaces, scrubbing & disinfecting restrooms, polishing woodwork and restock supplies.

Move-in and Move-out

Vacuuming and mopping floors. Wiping down baseboards. Dusting all surfaces and fixtures. Cleaning kitchen appliances and cabinets inside and out. Cleaning sinks, toilets, showers, and countertops.

Post-Construction Cleaning

Have a recent project or remodel scheduled? Don’t worry about the construction more than you have to. We will take care of the cleanup to make your house or workspace spotless.
